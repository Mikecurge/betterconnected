What this notice covers

This Cookie Notice explains what cookies are, how Better Connections Limited uses them on this website (better-connected.biz), and what choices you have in relation to their use. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

We take a straightforward approach to cookies: we use the minimum necessary to keep this website functioning, and we do not use advertising, tracking, or analytics cookies without your consent.

What cookies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember information about your visit — such as your preferences or whether you have accepted certain notices — for a period of time. Cookies set by us are called first-party cookies. Cookies set by other organisations whose content appears on our pages are called third-party cookies.

Cookies can be either session cookies, which are deleted when you close your browser, or persistent cookies, which remain on your device for a set period of time or until you delete them.

Essential cookies and your consent

Under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (PECR), cookies that are strictly necessary for a service you have requested do not require prior consent. The single cookie we currently set — which records your consent choice — is in this category, because without it we would need to show you the consent banner on every page.

Where we add any non-essential cookies in future, we will ask for your consent before setting them and give you a clear way to decline.
