Cookie Notice

Better Connections Limited · Effective date: 7 July 2026
Last reviewed: July 2026

What this notice covers

This Cookie Notice explains what cookies and similar technologies are, how Better Connections Limited (trading as Better Connected) uses them on this website (betterconnected.biz), and what choices you have. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

We take a straightforward approach. Essential storage keeps the site working. Google Analytics runs only if you explicitly consent through the banner. Nothing else.

What cookies and similar technologies are

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to remember information about your visit, such as your preferences or whether you have accepted certain notices, for a period of time. Cookies set by us are called first party cookies. Cookies set by other organisations whose content appears on our pages are called third party cookies.

This site also uses browser localStorage, a similar technology that stores small values on your device. The same rules and the same choices apply to it, so we list it in the table below alongside cookies.

Analytics and Google Consent Mode

With your consent, we use Google Analytics 4 (property G-4YLLQJ19L4) to understand how visitors use the site. It is loaded through Google Consent Mode, which works as follows:

  • By default, all analytics and advertising storage is set to denied. No Google Analytics script loads and no analytics cookies are set.
  • If you choose Accept on the banner, the Google Analytics script loads and analytics storage is granted. Advertising storage remains denied. We do not use advertising features.
  • If you choose Essential only, the Google Analytics script never loads and no analytics cookies are set.

We use analytics data in aggregate form only, to see which pages are read and how visitors move through the site. We do not use it to identify individuals and we do not combine it with other data for advertising or profiling.

What we do not use

We confirm that this website does not use any of the following:

  • Advertising or remarketing cookies
  • Social media tracking pixels
  • Third party behavioural profiling tools
  • Session recording or heatmap software

If this changes, we will update this Cookie Notice before implementing anything new and will obtain fresh consent where required under the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations 2003 (PECR).

Essential storage and your consent

Under PECR, storage that is strictly necessary for a service you have requested does not require prior consent. The two localStorage values we always set (your consent choice and your display preference) are in this category. Without them we would need to show you the consent banner and reset your display mode on every page.

Everything else, meaning Google Analytics, is set only after you give explicit consent via the banner.

Changing your choice

You can change your cookie choice at any time. Select Cookie settings in the footer of any page and the consent banner will reopen so you can choose again. Choosing Essential only stops any further analytics activity from that point. To remove analytics cookies that were set while consent was in place, delete cookies for this site in your browser settings.

Managing cookies in your browser

You can also control cookies through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies. Please note that blocking essential storage may affect the functionality of this website. For guidance on managing cookies in the most commonly used browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Apple Safari
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Microsoft Edge

Google also provides a browser add-on to opt out of Google Analytics across all websites: Google Analytics opt-out browser add-on.

Changes to this notice

We will update this Cookie Notice whenever our use of cookies or similar technologies changes materially. The effective date at the top of this page will be updated accordingly. We will display a fresh consent notice if we introduce any new non-essential cookies.
