Our methodology

Market intelligence with a structure behind it.

Market movements only matter if they change a decision. Our four stage process translates wholesale market data into a clear commercial recommendation for your specific position.

01

Anchor Report

A fixed reference point for your portfolio. Where your contracts sit against the market today.

02

Market Log

Continuous tracking of wholesale movements, policy changes and supplier behaviour that affect your position.

03

Insight Builder

Movements interpreted against your consumption profile, contract terms and renewal window.

04

Client Output

A clear commercial decision. Fix, wait, restructure or challenge, with the reasoning shown.

Proprietary methodology of Better Connections Limited
