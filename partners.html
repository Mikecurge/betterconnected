Our Partners

You have seen our badge on a website you trust.

The "Energy Strategy managed by Better Connected" badge appears on the websites of schools, trusts and organisations whose energy and carbon position we manage. This page explains what that means.

The badge is issued by us and placed with the organisation's agreement. It is not a paid advertisement.

What the badge means

An organisation displaying the badge has appointed Better Connected to manage its energy strategy. In practice this covers procurement and contract strategy, bill validation and error recovery, consumption monitoring and reporting written for governors, trustees and boards.

It signals that the organisation's energy position is professionally and independently managed, rather than left to renewal-day pricing.

Who we are

Better Connected is an advisory-led energy and carbon consultancy. We are not an energy broker. We work for the client, provide bespoke pricing and forensic contract auditing, and stay engaged for the life of the contract, not just the signature.
