Why we ask

Our service is built around named portfolio managers, bill by bill auditing and advisory support that runs for the life of every contract. That model works brilliantly for some organisations and is the wrong shape for others.

These five questions tell us enough to give you a straight answer. If we are not the right fit, we will say so and, where we can, point you somewhere more useful.

What happens next

A consultant reads your answers, not an algorithm. You get a reply either way. If you qualify, the reply explains what working with us would look like. If you do not, the reply says that plainly.
